2026 Roadmap

One excellent founding year.

Building deliberately. One strong proof-of-concept in Nairobi generates the credibility for everything that follows.

June 2026
Foundation & Identity

Name, domain, website live, social channels launched, core team confirmed, founding vision published.

July 2026
Content Engine Live

First 4 pieces of original content published; community seeded to 200+ followers; first partnerships opened.

July – August 2026
Partnerships Secured

2–3 institutional partners confirmed — university, corporate sponsor, and community organisation aligned with our mission.

August 2026
Conference Framework Finalised

Venue, agenda structure, speaker pipeline, and invite list for the Nairobi founding event confirmed.

September – October 2026
Founding Conference, Nairobi

One-day event: dialogue workshops in the morning, solutions showcase in the afternoon. The first Circle.

November 2026
Post-Event Report & Debrief

Impact report published; funding proposals for 2027 drafted; next host country selected.

Morning: Dialogue Workshops

Facilitated sessions exploring climate adaptation, community reconciliation, economic precarity, and mental health — rigorous, outcome-oriented, and designed to produce actionable frameworks.

Afternoon: Solutions Showcase

Curated introductions between youth-led ventures and invited investors, corporates, and ecosystem builders. The first step in building our continent-wide pipeline.
