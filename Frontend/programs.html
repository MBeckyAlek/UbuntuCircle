Our Programmes

What We Do

Two interconnected tracks — dialogue and enterprise — so every conversation about a problem creates a pathway to solving it.

Track 1

The Dialogue Platform

A digital and in-person community where young Africans engage with the continent’s defining challenges. Topics span climate resilience, mental health, conflict resolution, governance, and entrepreneurship.

The platform produces content — essays, interviews, discussions — documenting what African youth are thinking and building, distributed through our website and social channels rooted in Nairobi.

In-person, this takes the form of workshop-style sessions: how do divided communities reconcile? How do young people navigate economic precarity? How do we think about climate adaptation at a local level? The dialogue is rigorous, facilitated, and outcome-oriented.

Track 2

The Solutions Showcase

At each gathering, the dialogue culminates in a structured showcase where youth-led ventures, social enterprises, and innovative projects are presented to an invited audience of investors, corporates, and ecosystem builders.

This is not a standard pitch competition — it is a curated meeting between problem-solvers and the people positioned to support them.

Participants gain access to validation frameworks, peer feedback, and introductions to funding pathways. Over time, this showcase becomes a pipeline: a continuously updated database of African youth ventures serving as an intelligence layer for the broader investment ecosystem.
