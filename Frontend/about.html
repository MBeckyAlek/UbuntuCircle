Who We Are

About Ubuntu Circle

A Pan-African community platform that turns dialogue into action by connecting young leaders, innovators, mentors, and funders across the continent.

The Idea

Bridging dialogue and action.

African youth have been talking about their continent’s problems for a long time. Many have also been building solutions — in their neighbourhoods, their universities, their side projects.

What they rarely have is a structured space where dialogue converts into action, and where their ideas meet the people who can help them grow. Ubuntu Circle bridges that gap.

The name draws from the Southern African philosophy of Ubuntu — I am because we are. It reflects both the collective nature of the dialogue and the belief that African solutions are best built by Africans, together.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Vision

A continent where African youth-led solutions are discovered, developed, and supported — through dialogue that produces real outcomes and networks that last beyond any single event.

Mission

To bring young Africans together to discuss the issues that shape their lives, then create a direct pathway from that conversation to practical support: collaboration, mentorship, and access to funding ecosystems.

Our Approach

From conversation to impact.

Three connected steps that turn dialogue into lasting change.

01

Dialogue

Facilitated, outcome-oriented sessions — digital and in-person — where African youth engage rigorously with the continent’s defining challenges.

02

Collaborate

Peer feedback, mentorship connections, and validation frameworks that help problem-solvers refine their ideas and build trusted networks.

03

Showcase

Curated introductions between youth-led ventures and investors, corporates, and ecosystem builders — building a pipeline of African innovation.
